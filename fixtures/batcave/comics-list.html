The Comics List is the main catalog on BatCave.biz — a single index of all 33,462+ titles currently in the library, drawn from DC Comics, Marvel, Image Comics, Dark Horse, IDW, and a range of independent publishers. This section is the starting point for browsing by title, finding a specific series, or exploring what's available from a given publisher.

Finding Comics in the Catalog

The catalog is searchable by title and filterable by genre and publisher. For users who know what they want, the search bar returns results against the full title index — entering "Batman" pulls the main series, limited series, and spin-offs. Entering a creator name returns titles associated with that writer or artist across publishers.

Genre filtering narrows the catalog to specific categories: superhero, horror, sci-fi, crime, fantasy, war, and others. Publisher filtering lets you read DC Comics online in sequence, work through the Marvel catalog, or pull up the full Image Comics library independently. Both filters can be combined — for example, filtering to Image Comics + sci-fi returns titles like Saga and Prophet without scrolling through unrelated entries.

Series to Start With

The catalog covers titles across difficulty-of-entry and continuity complexity. Accessible starting points with complete or near-complete runs available:

  • Invincible (Image Comics) — 144-issue ongoing series, self-contained continuity, full run indexed
  • Saga (Image Comics) — currently ongoing, issues available from #1 through the current volume
  • Batman: Year One (DC Comics) — four-issue limited series, complete, no prior continuity required
  • Ultimate Spider-Man (Marvel) — 160-issue run with a clean entry point, full series available
  • X-Men: God Loves, Man Kills (Marvel) — standalone graphic novel, no tie-in reading required
  • Hellboy (Dark Horse) — episodic structure, most story arcs readable independently
  • Locke & Key (IDW) — six-volume limited series, complete run in catalog

Ongoing Series and New Issues

For readers following current publishing, the catalog includes ongoing series from DC and Marvel updated as new issues are added. Titles like Batman, X-Men, Spider-Man, and Absolute Wonder Woman are indexed with their full back-issue history alongside current issues. Each title page shows the complete issue list in order, so it's straightforward to find where a run currently stands and start from any point in the continuity.

Limited series and event titles are cataloged with their issue count marked, so readers can identify complete versus in-progress runs before starting. Trade paperbacks and collected editions appear in the same index as single issues, labeled by format.
