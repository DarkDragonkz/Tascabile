Over 33,462 comics from DC Comics, Marvel, Image Comics, Dark Horse, and IDW — available to read online, free, without creating an account. BatCave.biz indexes ongoing series, limited series, graphic novels, and trade paperbacks, updated regularly as new issues release.

What's in the Library

The catalog covers decades of output from the major publishers. DC Comics titles include long-running ongoing series like Batman, Superman, and Green Lantern, alongside landmark graphic novels such as Watchmen, The Dark Knight Returns, and Saga of the Swamp Thing. Marvel titles span the full continuity — X-Men, Spider-Man, Avengers, Daredevil — including both monthly ongoing series and collected trade paperbacks.

Independent publishers are represented in depth. Image Comics titles include Saga, Invincible, The Walking Dead, and Spawn. Dark Horse holdings cover Hellboy, Sin City, Usagi Yojimbo, and licensed properties. IDW adds Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Locke & Key, and a broad range of licensed comics. The library includes complete runs where available — not just recent issues.

Formats Available

The site hosts multiple publication formats under one roof:

  • Ongoing series — monthly titles updated as new issues are added, covering current storylines from DC, Marvel, and Image
  • Limited series — self-contained story arcs with a defined issue count, including event tie-ins and prestige format runs
  • Graphic novels — original standalone works published as single volumes, not serialized
  • Trade paperbacks — collected editions of previously serialized story arcs, useful for reading complete storylines in order
  • Annuals and specials — one-shots, crossover tie-ins, and anniversary issues cataloged alongside their parent series

How to Read Comics Online Here

No registration is required. Open any title, select an issue from the chapter list, and the reader loads in the browser. Navigation works issue-by-issue with forward and back controls. Search by title, character, publisher, or creative team. The Comics List section provides the full catalog sorted by title, with filtering by publisher and genre available for narrowing results.

New additions are indexed regularly — ongoing series receive new issues as they publish, and older catalog titles are added on an ongoing basis. To track a specific series, use the title page to see the full issue list and identify where the current run stands.
