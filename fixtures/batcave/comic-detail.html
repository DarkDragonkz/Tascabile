A new presence has arrived in Gotham City. A figure named Gotham, clad in a stark white uniform, now patrols the skies with a power that rivals the Dark Knight. This self-proclaimed hero operates under a simple, public mandate: to save the city from its own protector. For the first time, Batman faces an opponent who cannot be outmaneuvered in the shadows or broken by fear. The challenge is not criminal insanity, but a purer, more devastating form of justice. The streets below watch as their two guardians circle each other, a silent war for the city's soul fought in daylight and on live television.
