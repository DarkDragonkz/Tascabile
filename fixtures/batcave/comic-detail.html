The city’s superheroes are celebrated idols, but their private lives are a catalog of unchecked power and collateral damage. Wee Hughie learns this firsthand when a superhuman incident shatters his world. His recruitment brings him into the orbit of The Boys, a covert unit operating with CIA sanction to police these threats. The team is a collection of damaged specialists: the vengeful Butcher, the unnaturally sustained Mother’s Milk, the enigmatic Frenchman, and the silent, lethal The Female. They operate from the shadows, applying brutal pressure to a glossy, corrupt industry. Their work leaves stains on the pavement that no publicist can clean.
